Personal goal

Before entering Nanjing Foreign Language School British Columbia Academy department, I have been looking for one subject that I am passionate about. Through learning school mandatary curriculums, I found I was keen on studying biology. However, since the knowledge we learned is almost knowable from everyday life, I prefer gain those more specialized in spare time to make sure whether I am into biology. On the recommendation of a couple of seniors, they advised me to buy an entry-level access book about life science, which is written by the winner of Nobel Prize in Medicine and Physiology.

Briefly browsing the book two months ago, I set a long-term goal with time restriction based on the number of items on the catalogue. Since then, I have been working on it for approximately one month. Whenever I have day-off, I would like to spend time construing, searching keystone that author explain in each section. In the beginning, it was a bit difficult to comprehend those completely unfamiliar terms. After searching on a particular internet, the majority of questions can be answered. Nevertheless, when the internet cannot solve the problem, I will make an appointment with my school science teacher and discuss about it together, which greatly deepen my understanding and improve analytical skills.

During the period of reading and analyzing, my self-educated ability plays a vital role in exploring challenging field. As a result, the process I am undergoing provides me a win-win prospect, acquiring knowledge as well as skills training.
