Egg Drop Essay

Our final design was the result of many attempt. Before the finalize, we tried to protect the egg by using foam plastic inside the container. Our attention mainly focused on the infilling at the beginning of the experiment. However, after a few times failures of dropping, we found that the filler which can buffering pressure is not the keystone, whereas the conducts used outside the box is what matters. Based on preliminary conclusions, we changed the strategy. In order to make our design in a light and protective way, we chose to settle the foam stick glued on the surface of the container. Besides, different settlements of sticks can defend from all directions. Even though the imagination is well-rounded, but in reality we still encountered problems. It is difficult to make the foam stick adhere to the box tightly, and the rope is unstrained because of the restriction of the space. A couple of sticks divide the space into different section and that result in there is not sufficient interspace for hands to move. Fortunately, we altered our steps, the sticks were finally tied to the container successfully. Therefore, the ultimate design of our group initially formed.

After we first dropped the egg on the third floor, the egg itself remained intact. Whereas, the moment the container was thrown out the window, one of the foam plastic sticks outside our container broke off, and another one broke when it hit the ground. Analogously, after dropping the container on the fourth floor, two of them broke. The break of the container’s shield—foam sticks was expected, and we were hoping that the force applied to the egg would be converted to the force applied to the foam by a ring of foam sticks outside the container. I think we managed to pull it off and beat expectations. If we have a chance to do this experiment again I might pay more attention to the structure of the device. We would use a more rigid arrangement of foam sticks, such as a triangular structure to ensure stability or a “井” square structure on each side of the box so that the device would be protected to the maximum extent possible. The main thing is we need more foam sticks, then fasten them around the container.

During the dropping, the gravitational potential energy transformed into mechanical kinetic energy and then when the foam stick hit the ground, it deformed, so the mechanical energy transformed again into elastic potential energy. Since the stick was deformed beyond the strain that elastic deformation persists, the stress was no longer proportional to strain, or plastic deformation occurs, so part of the sticks’ shape were damaged .Moreover, elastic potential energy is too large to be contained in the foam, the stick broke into pieces which would consume energy, thus the energy that the container would receive is lower and meanwhile the egg could be protected well. In the whole process, the position of the egg is inside the box, and nearly only bear gravity. It took 3 seconds from the fourth floor to drop to the ground, therefore, according to the formular of velocity, it is estimate that the average velocity is 4m/s. Furthermore, the mass that measured before dropping is 71g in total, because of the centrifugal force, the box was moving faster and faster. As a result, depending on all the previous information, the acceleration is considered to be 9.8m/s2.
