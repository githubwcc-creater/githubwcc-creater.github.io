What is the major harm of white pollution?

“White pollution” refers to the image of life waste which comes from the usage of plastic products. However, it is difficult to dispose resulting in severe urban environmental consequences which are mainly visual pollution and potential hazards (Lambert 1).

The visual solution means plenty of plastic waste stewed directly into the natural environment without disposal those rubbish damage people’s stimulation and affect the entire beauty of nature (Lambert 2). For instance, when people see a mountain of waste thrown along the highway or, their mental and physical health will probably be damaged because of the overwhelming stench and disgusting picture.

One of the potential hazards is marine pollution. It is a combination of chemicals and trash, and most of them comes from land and is washed or thrown into the ocean. This pollution results in damage the environment, jeopardize all organisms’ health, and threaten worldwide economy (Admin 1). This trash poses dangers to both humans and animals. Fish are injured, and some animals mistake items like plastic bags for food and eat them. Small organisms feed on tiny bits of broken plastic, and absorb the chemicals from the plastic into their tissues.

Overall, the main hazards have great influence on creatures including both animals and human. The solutions are needed instantly to decrease the unbearable harm.
