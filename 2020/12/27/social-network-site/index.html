social network site

Numerous data has shown a trend of younger age in social network users. For example, Bilibili, a famous software among the youth, and half of its users are underage and even under 13. However, this phenomenon is not optimistic, and teenagers under 13 years old need to be restricted on social network site. More and more teenagers using social network sites result in wasting much more time and more cases of online froad.

The majority of the teenagers are lack of enough self-control, therefore, it is probable for them to waste too much time on social network sites. They are too small to set up a sense of time because of their little life experience and less understanding of the impact of wasting time. Although the adults have much more comprehension of better time management skills, the data reveals that the average time of adults spending on social network site everyday is about three hours. Thus, it is likely that children have less ability of resisting temptation from the network since some of them do not have enough awareness of self-discipline.

Due to the facts that teenagers do not have enough social cognition and ability of distinction, children can be cheated easily. Judging from a statistic of the age distribution of the deceived, teenaged internet users who lack sufficient social experience are the main target and victims of online fraud. Specifically, young users under 13 are the largest number of the cheated, accouting for 38.6% of the total. Therefore, it can be seen that teenagers become an important target of online scams. Also, they are lacking cautiousness so it is possible for them to let out their personal information uncousiously. That will cause the increasing burden of their family and even kidnap occasionally. As a result, allowing teenagers to use social network is similar to put them in danger gradually.

Considering the defects of people under 13, giving them permission does more harm than good. The drawbacks are not only their less work efficiency caused by spending time on social network site but also bringing them in danger. Therefore, administraters should prohibit the access. Besides, another method to reduce the detriment to the phenomenon of the tendency of younger users is asking their parents to do the regulation on social network site.
